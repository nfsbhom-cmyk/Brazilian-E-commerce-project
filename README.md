E-Commerce Sales Performance Analysis
📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to uncover key trends in revenue, customer behavior, and product performance. The goal was to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights through an interactive dashboard.
________________________________________
🎯 Business Objective
To evaluate overall sales performance and identify key drivers of revenue growth, customer engagement, and geographic trends.
________________________________________
📊 Dataset Description
The dataset consists of transactional e-commerce data, including:
•	Orders
•	Customers
•	Products
•	Order dates
•	Revenue values
The data spans from 2016 to 2018, enabling time-based trend analysis.
________________________________________
🛠 Data Preparation & Modeling
•	Cleaned data by removing duplicates and handling missing values
•	Created a Date Table to enable proper time intelligence analysis
•	Established relationships between tables using a star schema model
•	Ensured correct data types for all fields
________________________________________
📐 Key Metrics (DAX Measures)
•	Total Revenue – Sum of all sales transactions
•	Total Orders – Count of all orders placed
•	Total Customers – Distinct count of customers
•	Average Order Value (AOV) – Revenue per order
________________________________________
📈 Dashboard Overview
The dashboard includes:
•	KPI cards for high-level performance tracking
•	Revenue trend over time
•	Top product categories driving revenue
•	Geographic distribution of orders
•	Order growth trends
Interactive filters allow users to explore data by year.
________________________________________
🔍 Key Insights
•	Strong Revenue Growth
Revenue increased significantly from 2017 to 2018, indicating growing demand and business expansion.
•	Customer Purchasing Behavior
The ratio of orders to customers suggests that most customers placed a single order, highlighting an opportunity to improve customer retention.
•	Top Product Contribution
The top 3 product categories contribute over 60% of total revenue, showing a high dependency on a few key segments.
•	Geographic Concentration
Orders are heavily concentrated in one state, indicating potential geographic risk and opportunities for market expansion.
________________________________________
💡 Business Recommendations
•	Improve Customer Retention
Introduce loyalty programs or targeted marketing to increase repeat purchases.
•	Diversify Product Revenue
Expand and promote lower-performing categories to reduce reliance on top segments.
•	Expand Geographic Reach
Focus marketing efforts on underperforming regions to balance revenue distribution.
________________________________________
🎨 Tools Used
•	Power BI (Data modeling & dashboard creation)
•	DAX (Measures and calculations)
•	Excel (Initial data exploration)
________________________________________
🚀 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data visualization and modeling can transform raw data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making. The dashboard provides a clear and interactive way to monitor performance and identify growth opportunities.
